Filmmaker Michael König from Cologne, Germany has created an amazing video showing solar activity, made by stitching together footage from Nasa's Solar Dynamics Observatory over five years. The video includes plasma raining down on the sun, an extreme solar eruption and even a comet breezing through the sun’s atmosphere. By watching the sun in different wavelengths, scientists can watch how material courses through the corona, which holds clues to what causes eruptions and why the sun's magnetic fields are constantly on the move.

Kelly Parsons, a 35-year-old dental nurse from London, has donated 50 of her eggs to help childless couples conceive. She injected herself twice a day with drugs to stop her period and stimulate her ovaries, and after a few months she will have stored up enough for them to be harvested. In all, Kelly has donated eggs three times, leading to three successful pregnancies, and a number of them have been frozen for future use.
