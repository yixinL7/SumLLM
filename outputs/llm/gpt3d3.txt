A new video created by filmmaker Michael König from Cologne, Germany has stitched together footage from Nasa's Solar Dynamics Observatory over five years, revealing the beauty of the sun's surface as it bursts with energy. Highlights of the video include solar prominences, active regions on the sun's surface, transits of the moon, Venus and Earth, and Comet Lovejoy passing the sun. By watching the sun in different wavelengths, scientists can observe material coursing through the corona, which holds clues to what causes eruptions on the sun and what heats the sun's atmosphere.
